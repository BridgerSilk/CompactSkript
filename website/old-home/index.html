Compact Skript

v0.0.1

Compact Skript 0.0.1 We're thrilled to announce the launch of the very first public beta version, v0.0.1, of Compact Skript! Built upon the robust foundation of Skript, Compact Skript introduces a streamlined and efficient programming experience with a plenty of unique features and an easy to learn syntax. Currently Compact Skript can save up to 60% storage compared to Skript, due to the optimized and simplified syntax.

Exciting Updates Ahead!

Get ready for exciting updates in Compact Skript! We're introducing completely custom syntax never seen before in Skript, along with support for importing addons directly into your Compact Skript files. Stay tuned for these game-changing features!

Storage Efficiency

With Compact Skript, you can save up to 60% on storage compared to traditional Skript. Our streamlined syntax ensures that your scripts take up minimal space, allowing you to optimize your server resources and focus on creating amazing gameplay experiences.

Seamless Integration

Compact Skript is seamlessly integrated with Minecraft servers through the Compact Skript plugin. Enjoy all the features of Skript with our compact syntax, ensuring smooth and efficient script execution for your server.
