About Compact Skript

Compact Skript is a revolutionary programming language designed specifically for Minecraft server development. It's the result of the collaborative efforts of a small team of dedicated developers, including BridgerSilk, focused on providing server owners and developers with a powerful yet easy-to-use scripting language that enhances gameplay and customization possibilities.

Compact Skript stands out with its compact syntax, offering significant storage savings compared to traditional Skript while maintaining full compatibility and functionality. Accompanied by comprehensive documentation, this language is accessible to developers of all skill levels.

Our Team

Being an open-source project, Compact Skript welcomes contributions from the community. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, there are plenty of opportunities to get involved and shape the future of Minecraft server development.

Join this exciting journey as we continue to evolve and improve Compact Skript, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in Minecraft scripting. Together, we can unleash the full potential of server customization and take Minecraft gameplay to new heights.
